About the Academy

The academy has expertise, education, and computer software all for the purpose of supporting positive workplace practices. Expertise, education, and software already developed is through traditional sales and marketing and with partners. Processes, methods and software under development involves company partners as consortium members. They participate in some way in the development of new products.

A consortium is an association of two or more individuals, companies, organizations or governments (or any combination of these entities) with the objective of participating in a common activity or pooling their resources for achieving a common goal.

A consortium is assembled for each new development project and there is a variable fee to participate. Benefits to a participating company are (1) influence design and functionality (2) contractual resale rights of final product and (3) discounted license fees for internal use.

Workplace Team Behaviors

Our specialty is working with business teams to arrive at the behaviors expected of its members and then measuring their achievement of those behaviors. We use the World Cafe process to guide the team to arrive at and define their behaviors. Then the software for teams collects data to measure achievement of those behaviors. We have designed and documented the product. This project is ready for a support consortium to be assembled.

Document Company Culture Practices

The “field” of business culture is so broad that it is nearly impossible for a company to be aware of the many possible practices. We have designed and documented an interactive system for companies to document their existing culture practices and then share them with all employees. The system also shows what other practices and tools a company might consider. This project is ready for a support consortium to be assembled.

Workplace Wisdom Retreat

The Academy sponsors the prestigious Workplace Wisdom retreat. This is a group of culture professionals who are together in support of a common cause. The cause is to share expertise on how to create and sustain positive workplace cultures. The interactive format with all participants including the speakers is something that isn’t seen anywhere else. It emphasizes friendships with like-minded people and taking home fresh new implementable ideas.

City Chapters

The Academy has chapters in Omaha, NE, Kansas City, MO, Dallas, TX and Denver, CO.  Chapters arrange 2-4 local meetings per year with speakers.  Each Chapter has a President with a leadership team.   The purpose of the chapters is education that promotes practices for positive workplace cultures. We have a set of guidelines for starting new chapters. Its purpose is to make it simpler to start new chapters.

Words of Wisdom

Each month we feature a prominent leader giving “words of wisdom” for creating and maintaining positive workplace cultures. Viewers can add their own words of wisdom which will be published the following month.

Company Culture Gardening

A few months ago, I thought about company culture being like a family vegetable garden. Both need nurturing, cultivation, fertilizer, sunshine, water and the plants that will produce the desired result. Then I had the good fortune to be introduced to Dr. Irv Rubin. He liked the idea. Now he and I and a few guest authors are writing chapters for a book to be called “A Company Culture Gardeners Guide”. We will publish them one chapter at a time. When finished we’ll put them into a book. If you would like to suggest a chapter that you would like to write, let Jerry know. Or maybe you know someone that would like to. The introductory chapter here introduces “Company Culture Gardens”.

— Jerry Wagner.
